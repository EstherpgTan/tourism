Tasmania's north west is the gateway to some of Tasmania's most beautiful natural places.


Home to Cradle Mountain experience forest adventures, great walks and some of the best scenery in the world.


In Tasmania's west coast, the Franklin river runs through the Tasmanian Wilderness World Heritage area, one of the world's last great wild rivers. It forges through the rugged south west of Tasmania, through deep gorges, quiet pools and temperate rainforests.
